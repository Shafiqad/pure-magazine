Pure Flight

Luxury travel without compromise.

Travel Aviation Issue 01
On the tarmac · The journey begins before the engines do

In a world where time has become one of life’s most valuable assets, the way we travel is changing. Comfort is no longer seen as an indulgence, but as an expectation. For entrepreneurs, athletes, public figures and frequent travellers, the journey itself has become just as important as the destination.

That is where Pure Flight comes in.

Founded by Omar Mahran and Anass Oakki, Pure Flight was created from a simple but powerful observation: many travellers want to experience the comfort of business class or first class, but are often held back by the high fares they find online. Pure Flight set out to change that — by making premium air travel more accessible, without compromising on service, comfort or experience.

What started as a shared passion for travel has grown into a service-driven company operating in the world of luxury travel. But unlike traditional booking platforms, Pure Flight looks beyond the ticket. Much further.

“
We noticed that many clients wanted to fly business or first class, but would drop off as soon as they saw the online prices. That is when we realised there was a real opportunity. People want comfort, convenience and service — at fair and transparent rates.

Omar Mahran · Co-founder

Together with co-founder Anass Oakki, he built Pure Flight into a personal travel partner for a growing network of entrepreneurs, professional athletes, well-known personalities and frequent travellers who value quality, discretion and direct communication.

Omar Mahran & Anass Oakki Co-founders · Pure Flight

More Than a Ticket

What sets Pure Flight apart is not only access to premium fares, but the way the entire process is handled.

From the first request to the moment the boarding pass is sent, every step is personally managed. No endless comparison websites. No long waiting times. No anonymous customer service.

Clients communicate directly via WhatsApp and receive tailored flight options based on their destination, schedule, preferences and budget. From there, Pure Flight takes care of the full journey — sourcing the best available business class and first class deals, handling the booking, managing the check-in and sending the boarding pass directly to the client.

“
We believe in long-term relationships, not quick transactions. Trust and reliability are more important to us than simply making a booking.

Anass Oakki · Co-founder

It is this personal approach that has allowed Pure Flight to build strong relationships with a demanding clientele. For business owners and athletes in particular, flexibility, privacy and time-saving are not luxuries. They are necessities.

The Rise of Premium Travel

The demand for premium travel continues to grow. Within Pure Flight, destinations in the Middle East and the United States are currently among the most requested routes. Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Doha remain especially popular, supported by partnerships with various airlines that allow Pure Flight to offer exclusive rates.

At the same time, the company is seeing a strong increase in requests for American destinations such as New York, Miami and Las Vegas.

And that comes as no surprise.

Today, business class is no longer only about extra space or a better seat. It is about arriving rested, saving time at the airport, having access to exclusive lounges, enjoying more privacy in the air, and experiencing a smoother journey from departure to arrival.

For frequent travellers, that difference matters.

“
Even people who usually fly economy are becoming more curious about what business class actually feels like. Our goal is to make that experience more reachable — without people having to pay extreme prices.

Omar Mahran · Co-founder

From the Netherlands and the UAE to the World

Pure Flight currently operates from the Netherlands and the United Arab Emirates — two markets that naturally connect business, travel and international lifestyle. But the ambition reaches far beyond that.

With plans to expand towards Asia and the United States, Pure Flight is building a global network for the modern traveller: internationally minded, time-conscious and used to a certain level of quality.

Still, the essence of the company remains the same.

Personal contact. Tailored service. Complete peace of mind.

Coda

Because in the end, luxury travel is not only about where you are going.

It is about how the journey begins.
